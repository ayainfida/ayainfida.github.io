I am a MPhil student in Advanced Computer Science at the University of Cambridge, where I am working under the supervision of Prof. Mateja Jamnik.

Previously, I graduated with a BS Computer Science degree from the Lahore University of Management Sciences, where I was fortunate to be advised by Dr. Zafar Ayyub Qazi and Dr. Ihsan Ayyub Qazi.

My research interests lie at the intersection of networking systems and machine learning. I am keen on designing real-world, scalable systems that are not only efficient but also trustworthy and socially responsible.
